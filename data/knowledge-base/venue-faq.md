# Venue FAQ (RAG source — sample)

## Entry & Gates
- Gates open 3 hours before kickoff. Gate A (North) and Gate C (East) are the
  main pedestrian entrances. Gate C has step-free access and priority lanes for
  guests requiring assistance.

## Accessibility
- Step-free routes connect all concourse levels via elevators near Gate C.
- Sensory-friendly quiet rooms are located on Concourse Level 1.
- Assistance can be requested from any information point or via the app.

## Amenities
- Water refill stations are available on every concourse level (sustainability).
- Recycling and compost stations are located beside all food courts.

## Transport
- The metro station is a 6-minute step-free walk from Gate A.
- Accessible shuttle drop-off is at the East plaza near Gate C.
- Park-and-ride lots run continuous shuttles from 3 hours before kickoff.
